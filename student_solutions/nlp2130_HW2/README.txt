TwoStackQueue.java
To compile: javac TwoStackQueue.java 
To run: Run Program2 

This generic class builds a queue out of two completely separate stacks. Enqueue operations happen by
pushing items onto stack 1 and dequeue operations happen by popping items from stack 1 and pushing those
onto stack 2. Subsequently, items are popped from stack 2.
